Program framing

This hub connects a broader research agenda on how agentic and generative AI affect trust, disinformation, verification, provenance, and cognitive resilience on the Web.

The central idea is simple: as synthetic content becomes harder to distinguish from authentic content, trust must increasingly be supported by infrastructure rather than intuition alone.
